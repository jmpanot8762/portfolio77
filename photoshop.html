Photoshop Editing

Image editing, poster design, and digital artwork creation were part of our hands-on learning experience during our training, where we were taught how to properly use Adobe Photoshop under the guidance of our teacher, Sir Laurence Umali. He introduced us to the fundamental tools and techniques needed in professional image editing, such as layering, cropping, color correction, background removal, and blending effects. Through his instruction, we learned how to manipulate images carefully and creatively to produce high-quality outputs that are visually appealing and meaningful. We were also trained to design posters and digital artworks by applying principles of layout, balance, typography, and visual hierarchy. Sir Laurence Umali emphasized the importance of creativity combined with technical skill, teaching us how to turn simple images into professional-looking designs using Photoshop tools effectively. This experience helped us understand how digital artwork is created in real-world applications, especially in media, advertising, and content creation. Overall, it improved our confidence, creativity, and technical ability in using Adobe Photoshop as a powerful tool for digital design.
